The Labyrinth Stone of Laku Village

The labyrinth stone of Laku Village is one of the local tourist destinations that offers natural scenery with a background of karst hills. On an area of ​​approximately one hectare, clusters of rocks of all sizes stand firmly to form a garden that looks well maintained. In addition, the location is quite strategic and can be accessed through two routes, namely by land using a vehicle or simply walking, as well as water transportation by boat for 3 minutes from pier I Rammang – Rammang.
