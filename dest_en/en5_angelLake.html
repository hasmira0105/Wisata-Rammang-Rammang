The Angel Lake

Another one that should not be missed when visiting Rammang Rammang is a small paradise that hidden behind karst rocks. This natural pool is known as The Angel Lake. According to local people's beliefs, this place is the path of the angels, the reason being the appearance of the rainbow on the surface of the water and the karst walls are believed to be the way to earth. There is also a folklore that says, if you travel to this place and meet a known opposite sex, then you fall into the lake accidentally, it means that the door to your mate is open.
