Fireflies Cave

This place, known as the Fireflies Cave, is one of the other unique things that are preserved in the Rammang Rammang Karst Area. When entering this place, we will be treated to views of stalactites, stalagmites and colums that are hundreds of years old. And uniquely, when the rocks are exposed to light, a sparkling light will appear that sparkles like hundreds of fireflies that surround the walls in the cave. However, in order to enter this magical cave, visitors must bring equipment such as flashlights and wear shoes because the area in the cave is a bit slippery and dark.
